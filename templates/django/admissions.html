Admissions Process

Shri Ambika Sanskrit Mahavidyalaya admits dedicated students eager to embrace a rigorous traditional Vedic lifestyle. We have strict intake limits to ensure personalized attention for every single Rishi Kumar.

{# Criteria #}

❖ Eligibility Requirements

  • 1. For Standard 9: Must have successfully completed 8th grade. Age strictly between 13-15 years.
  • 2. Absolute willingness to follow the Gurukul's lifestyle, including pure vegetarianism, early morning rising (Brahma Muhurta), and daily Sandhyavandanam.
  • 3. Prior knowledge of basic Sanskrit chanting is highly preferred but not strictly mandatory.

Application Timeline

Forms Open Starts Chaitra Navaratri
Interviews Throughout May
Session Start June 1st Week
{# Interview & Registration #}

The Selection Interview

Our admission is not determined solely by previous academic marks. The Mahavidyalaya conducts an in-person interview with both the aspiring student and the parents/guardians.

The Principal Acharya evaluates the student's moral aptitude, discipline, retention capability (Smriti), and true passion for Sanskrit. Submitting an application form is merely the first step.
